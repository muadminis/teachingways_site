We believe in helping autism youth to attain the best possible life experience. We hope to see them in believing themselves when they reach to adulthood.

We believe in life long relationship and not transactional relationship. Via our teaching and mentoring, we hope to be our students’ teachers and also their lifelong coach. In the professional world, there is a term called trusted advisor and our goal is to be the trusted advisor for our students after they enter into their professional career.

We are a California based Public Benefit corporation (application pending). Our public mission is to serve the autism youth community while maintaining the commitment to our shareholders.
